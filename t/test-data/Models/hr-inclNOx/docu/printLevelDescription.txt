Print Level Description
-----------------------

0 = all
1 = Emissions after Level (yard summarized in housing)
2 = Emissions after Level, including yard
3 = Flux after Level 

5 = Add's Emission after Level and animal, storage or application category
6 = Animal Numbers
7 = Flux after Level and animal, storage or application category

10 = Emissionrates or emission Factors
15 = other Correction factors and parameters
