AGRAMMON Login

Authentication

You need an account to use AGRAMMON. Please enter your Username and Password and press the Login button.

Account creation

If you don't have an AGRAMMON account you can use the Create New Account button to create an account.

You will be asked to enter some information about yourself. After submission of this information an activation key will be eMailed to you. Enter this key as verification of your eMail address. Your account will then be activated and you can login immediately.

Password reset

If you already have an AGRAMMON account but forgot your password you can use the Reset Password button to set a new one.

You will be asked to enter your eMail address (username) and a new password. After submission of this information an activation key will be eMailed to you. Enter this key as verification of your identity. Your account will then be re-activated and you can login immediately with the new password.

Privacy

Your eMail address will ONLY be used for sending you information about AGRAMMON.

Support

Please send eMail to support@agrammon.ch if you have any questions about AGRAMMON.
